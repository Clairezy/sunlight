About

I am a graduate student studying the connection between lithium and stellar rotation in young populations. My work combines observational data with data analysis & visualization to constrain physical models and interpret statistical tests. I care deeply about scientific communication, outreach, and lifelong learning.

Research Overview

My current and past projects include:

  • Lithium-rotation connection for young stars within the Upper Scorpius region.
  • Age dating TOI 7032, a peculiar host star that may help us understand more on the origins of life.
  • Bayesian data analysis of an Open Cluster to study the dynamical evolution of its stellar members.
